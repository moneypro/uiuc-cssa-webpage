About CSSA at UIUC

The Largest Chinese Student Community in the Central U.S.

The Chinese Students and Scholars Association (CSSA) is the official organization for overseas Chinese students and scholars at UIUC. As the largest registered student organization in UIUC with 352 enthusiastic members, CSSA is dedicated to promote intellectual, social, and cultural activities among Chinese students and scholars as well as the U of I community.

Each semester we organized several dozen events. Some highlights from the past Fall Semester are:

CSSA Moon Gala

1,500 people enjoyed this 5-hour long show in Foellinger Auditorium. Provost of UIUC, Consul General of China in Chicago, and many other high profile guests and professors attended the event;

CRRC Seminar

The seminar was hosted in collaboration with China Railway Rolling Stock Corporation (CRRC; largest train builder in the world; Number of Employees: 175,700) and China General Chamber of Commerce - USA. It has an audience of 200 people, including many high profile guests such as the Dean and Associate Dean of the College of Engineering, Vice President of CRRC, President of Bank of China America Branch, Consul General of China in Chicago, etc;

International Career Forum

In collaboration with Business Career Service (BCS), Engineering Career Service (ECS) and Career Center, we hosted a job fair that targeted on international students and brought job opportunities to hundreds of students. For a very long time, UIUC CSSA has obtained great reputation and close relation with the university, as well as considerable influence over Chinese and international population and the entire university
