Keynote Speakers

Prof. Weng Cho Chew

Weng Cho Chew is George and Ann Fisher Distinguished Professor of Engineering at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.

Previously, he served as the Dean of Faculty of Engineering at the University of Hong Kong, and the first Chair Professor of Electrical and Computer Engineering at UI. Academically, Prof. Chew was widely known for his work in Electromagnetics and Optics. He is an IEEE fellow, OSA fellow, and IOP fellow.

Prof. Steven M. LaValle

Steven M. LaValle is a Professor, Angel Investor and Corporate Advisor at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.

Formerly, he served as the Principal Scientist of Oculus Rift, which was acquired by Facebook for $2 billion in 2014. Academically, he is best known for his work on RRT's and his book Planning Algorithms, one of the most highly cited texts in the field.
